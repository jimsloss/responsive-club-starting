Pool Club, Bar lounge and Diner
All you need for a great nights entertainment


Welcome to Dungannon Pool Club

Situation in the hub of tyrone, Dungannon Pool Club has 14 tables, a mix of full sized 9ft American 8 ball tables along with 7ft and 8ft standard English tables. Our tables and cues are kept well maintained so that you get the most out of your game.

Come and enjoy a few games with your friends. If you have been playing for a while you might like to join one of our teams, and if not then we offer one on one coaching sessions, if you would like to improve your game.

We are more than just Pool

We also have a bar and lounge, with three 70 inch plasma TV screens to relax and enjoy other televised sporting events.

Ask at reception for booking availability.

Coaching Sessions

Private one on one coaching sessions are available in half hour sessions.

Provided by our inhouse Coach Puel ball, two times UK Pool champion.
